Risk Assessment

Risks, Mitigations, and Their BCR Impact

Every risk worth naming, named — including the financial impact if it materialises.

Risk Factor Probability Mitigation BCR Impact if Risk Hits
Extended Ramp (6 wks vs 3) Moderate Stack already in production. FastAPI + React + OpenAI used in shipped projects. 2-week ramp is supported by prior context; 6-week is the pessimistic ceiling. −0.4× BCR → 2.3×
Senior Recovery Lower Than Expected Moderate Milestone-based delivery and async review cycles are designed to minimise synchronous dependency. Recovery modelled at 5 hrs/week; bear case uses 3 hrs/week. −0.6× BCR → 2.1×
Senior Rate Assumption Wrong Moderate ₹400/hr based on Glassdoor Mumbai PM median. If real blended rate is ₹300/hr, benefit drops by ~₹32K. BCR falls to ~2.5× — still positive. −0.2× BCR → 2.5×
Academic Schedule Conflict Moderate Milestone delivery structure — not fixed hours. Exam periods declared upfront. No net output impact if communicated 2 weeks in advance. Negligible if managed

Decision

Recommendation

The conclusion, with nothing softened.

The base case generates a 2.7× return on a bounded, front-loaded cost. The bear case still clears breakeven at 1.4×. The candidate's stack familiarity shortens ramp meaningfully, the deliverable methodology is shown in full, and the conversion option — while excluded from the headline BCR — represents real, probability-weighted value. The single honest caveat: the senior hourly rate assumption is the most sensitive variable in the model. If your blended rate is closer to ₹250/hr, the base BCR drops to ~2.2×. Still positive. Still a clear proceed.
